Nicola tesla (1856-1943)


A little bit of Nicola Tesla

He was a Serbian-American engineer and physicist who made dozens of breakthroughs in the production, transmission and application of electric power.

Tesla was born in Smiljan, Croatia, on July 10, 1856.

His father was a priest in the Serbian Orthodox church and his mother managed the family’s farm.

Things probably you did not know

Tesla designed the alternating-current (AC) electric system, which is still the predominant electrical system used across the world today.

Tesla also created the "Tesla coil," which is still used in radio technology.

Tesla and Thomas Edison grew to be fierce competitors, but in the end, Edison had the upper hand during their lifetimes.
